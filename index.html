Rules & Guides

Getting Started with KONIVRER

  • Each player starts with 30 life points
  • Draw 5 cards to form your starting hand
  • Play one card per turn unless specified otherwise
  • Elemental combinations create powerful synergies
  • Victory conditions: Reduce opponent's life to 0 or deck them out

Advanced Mechanics

  • Chain Reactions: Cards that trigger off other cards
  • Transmutation: Converting one element to another
  • Resonance: Bonus effects when multiple cards share elements
  • Temporal Shifts: Manipulating turn order and timing
  • Void Manipulation: Banishing and retrieving cards

Tournament Format

  • Swiss rounds followed by top 8 elimination
  • Best of 3 matches with sideboard allowed
  • 60-minute time limit per match
  • Banned and restricted card lists apply
  • Judge calls and appeal procedures

Deck Construction

  • Minimum 40 cards, maximum 60 cards
  • Maximum 4 copies of any single card
  • At least one card from each of 3 different elements
  • Sideboard of exactly 15 cards for tournament play
  • Consider mana curve and win conditions
