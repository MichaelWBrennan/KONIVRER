Classical Era
Summary
• Philosophy is mercy for confused minds.
• Empire is a road that apologizes by bringing bread.
• In the valley, both kneel before weather.

On Schools
Stoics practiced keeping promises to themselves. Epicureans practiced keeping promises to their friends. Cynics kept no promises and lived truthfully enough to be forgiven.

On Empire
Roads, aqueducts, law. All excellent until they forgot who they were for. In the valley the roads go where apologies are needed most.
