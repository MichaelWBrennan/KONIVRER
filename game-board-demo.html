Power System

• ⭐ Power = Generic Cost Paid + Base Power
• ⊗ Generic Cost Requirement (minimum to play)
• Players choose how much generic cost to pay when playing Elementals
• Higher cost = Higher power (strategic resource allocation)
• Single stat for both attack and defense

Deck Construction Rules

• 1 Flag (doesn't count toward total)
• 40 cards total: 25 Common, 13 Uncommon, 2 Rare
• Maximum 1 copy per card
• Elements: Fire, Water, Earth, Air, Quintessence, Void, Brilliance, Submerged
